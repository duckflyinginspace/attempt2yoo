Frequency

Recommended mapping: distance from the sun (in AU) -> Hz
Recommended range: 200-4000 Hz (the so-called Midrange, where most sounds and music we hear in our daily lives vibrate)


Volume

Recommended mapping: Density (in average gm/cm^3) -> Db
Recommended range: 30-90Db (where most daily sounds lie in)
