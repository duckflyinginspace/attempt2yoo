Welcome! Are you up for a musical challenge?

Here's how this website works:
Through sonification, the site converts specific planetary data (for example, mass or distance from the sun) to the necessary characteristics of a note (for example, pitch). This way, you, the user, can generate full notes directly from raw data, and then challenge yourself to write a track only with said notes!
Interested? Click the button on your left to make your own track!
Otherwise, browse tracks made by other users.
